Clearing a hotkey.

Clearing a hotkey setting is a one-click process.

To clear a hotkey:

  1. Find the hotkey field you wish to clear.
  2. Click on the "X" on the right-hand side of the field.
  3. The field will now display "Click to record shortcut". This means that the hotkey has been cleared.
