Pot betting problems

Q: Sometimes BlazingStars puts in incorrect values when I use the pot-betting hotkeys. How can I fix this?

A: BlazingStars uses image-processing technology to read the size of the pot at the top of the poker window, because PokerStars does not report this programmatically. In rare cases, the pot size might be read wrong if BlazingStars cannot get get an accurate picture of the pot size box, and this will lead to an incorrect calculation of the appropriate bet size for a pot-betting hotkey.

To solve pot betting problems:

  1. Increase the window size. A larger window size increases the accuracy of the image-processing routines BlazingStars uses.
  2. Switch to a different theme. Hyper-Simple and Black are good choices.
  3. E-mail me at support@fullyfunctionalsoftware.com if these steps do not resolve your problem.
